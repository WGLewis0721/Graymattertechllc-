FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the questions I get asked most before starting a project.

How long does it take to build my website?
Timelines depend on the plan and complexity. The Starter plan typically takes 5–7 business days, the Professional plan takes 7–14 days, and Premium / Full Platform builds take 2–4 weeks. Turnaround also depends on how quickly you provide content (copy, photos, branding materials).
Do I need to provide my own content and images?
For best results, yes — your own photos and copy always perform better. However, if you don't have content ready, I can use professional stock photography and you can optionally add the copywriting add-on. We'll discuss what you have available during our initial consultation.
What do I need to get started?
To kick off your project I'll need: a 50% deposit, your branding assets (logo, colors if available), any content you have, and a clear idea of what you want your site to do. No tech knowledge required — I'll guide you through everything step by step.
Will I be able to update my site myself after it's built?
Yes. Every site I deliver comes with a brief walkthrough of how to make basic updates — swapping images, editing text, adding new content. If you'd prefer a completely hands-off experience, the monthly maintenance add-on covers all updates for you.
Do you offer payment plans?
The standard payment structure is 50% upfront and 50% on delivery. For Premium / Full Platform builds over $800, I'm open to discussing a payment plan split into three installments. Reach out and let's make it work for your budget.
